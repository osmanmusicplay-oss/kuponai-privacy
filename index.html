Privacy Policy

Last updated: March 2026

KuponAI ("the App") does not collect any personally identifiable information. The App does not require account creation or the entry of personal data.

1. Data We Collect

The App temporarily caches daily coupon data locally on your device (AsyncStorage) to improve loading speed. This data:

  • Is never sent to any server by the App itself.
  • Is not user-specific; all users see the same content.
  • Is stored only to make the App open faster.

2. User Identity and Accounts

The App does not include any user registration, login, or personal account system. We do not request or store names, email addresses, phone numbers, or similar personal information.

3. Third‑Party Services

The App uses the following third‑party services to generate its content. These services are subject to their own privacy policies:

  • API‑Football – Match, odds, and statistics data (api-football.com/privacy)
  • Anthropic Claude API – AI‑powered match analysis (anthropic.com/privacy)
  • Supabase – Server‑side data storage (supabase.com/privacy)

4. Advertising and Tracking

The App does not include any advertising networks or user tracking technologies (analytics, tracking pixels, etc.).

5. Age Restriction

Because the App provides betting predictions, it is intended only for users who are 18 years of age or older. Minors are not allowed to use the App.

6. Disclaimer

The predictions provided by KuponAI are generated by artificial intelligence and are for informational purposes only. The App does not encourage gambling. The accuracy of predictions is not guaranteed. Users are fully responsible for any betting decisions they make.

7. Changes to This Policy

This privacy policy may be updated from time to time. Any changes will be published on this page. Your continued use of the App after changes are made constitutes acceptance of the updated policy.

8. Contact

If you have any questions about this privacy policy, please contact us at: kuponai.app@gmail.com
